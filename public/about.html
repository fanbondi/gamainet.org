About AI-GAMNET

Building a sustainable pan-African community of AI expertise through research, innovation, and collaboration.

Our mission

Advancing AI for Gambian and African development

AI-GAMNET conducts flagship events including Deep Learning IndabaX Gambia — locally organised conferences that bring together experts, researchers, professionals, and students to discuss the latest in AI and machine learning.

We strengthen our community and build capacity in fields that matter for Gambian development — from agriculture and health to education and governance.
